Hebcal Interactive Jewish Calendar

Hebcal.com offers a free personalized Jewish calendar for any year 0001-9999. You can get a list of Jewish holidays, candle lighting times, and Torah readings. We also offer export to Microsoft Outlook, Apple iCal, Google, and Palm.
