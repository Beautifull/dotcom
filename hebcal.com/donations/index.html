Donations to hebcal.com

Hebcal.com is a free Jewish calendar and holiday web site. It includes a powerful interactive Jewish calendar form that lets you generate a list of Jewish holidays for any year (past, present or future). Also available are Shabbat candle lighting times and Torah readings, a page to look up yahrzeits, birthdays and anniversaries, and a Hebrew date converter.

This is a free service. If you'd like to help defray the cost of our Internet hosting bill, you can make a donation to Michael J. Radwin.
