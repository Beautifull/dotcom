Importing Hebcal calendar data into Apple iCal

Apple iCal [#ical]

Here is how to subscribe to an iCalendar ICS file from hebcal.com:

  1. Go to http://www.hebcal.com/hebcal/
  2. Fill out the form with your preferences and click the "Get Calendar" button
  3. Click on the "Export calendar to Outlook, Apple iCal, Google, Palm, etc." link
  4. Click on the "Apple iCal" link
  5. Click on the "Subscribe to: Jewish Calendar YEAR.ics" link (see screenshot below)
  6. Apple iCal will start up
  7. Click Subscribe in the "Calendar URL:" dialog box (see screenshot below)
  8. Click OK in the next dialog box (see screenshot below)

If you're looking for a 10-year iCal feed, see also our Jewish Calendar downloads for Apple iCal page.

Deleting a calendar from Apple iCal [#ical-del]

You can delete any of the calendars or groups of calendars in your Calendar list. For example, if you want to stop subscribing to a calendar or stop viewing a calendar someone shared with you, delete it. Deleting a calendar removes the calendar and all the events it contains from your computer.

To delete a calendar:

  1. Select the calendar (or calendar group) you want to delete.
  2. Choose Edit &gr; Delete.

iPhone [#iphone]

Here is how to subscribe to a Hebcal calendar directly from your iPhone (running iPhone OS 3.0 or higher):

  1. Go to http://www.hebcal.com/hebcal/
  2. Fill out the form with your preferences and click the "Get Calendar" button
  3. Click on the "Export calendar to Outlook, Apple iCal, Google, Palm, etc." link
  4. Click on the "Apple iCal" link
  5. Click on the "Subscribe to: Jewish Calendar YEAR.ics" link to begin downloading the data
  6. When the Subscribe to the calendar "Hebcal YEAR"? dialog box appears, click >Subscribe
  7. When iPhone has finished subscribing, click Done
  8. If you later wish to remove it from your phone, go to over to Settings > Mail, Contacts, Calendar. You will see all your subscriptions listed under Accounts. Choose the one you want to edit or remove, and hit Delete Account
